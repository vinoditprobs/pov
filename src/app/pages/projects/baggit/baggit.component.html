This was an exercise in reinforcing Baggit’s credentials as a fashion accessories brand. A brand that provides its audiences a platform where they can ‘play’ any part they want. Through life’s twists, turns, glories and shortcomings, Baggit is an ally for our audience when they want to express themselves.

  • Strategy
  • Identity
  • Digital
  • Retail
  • Communications

In a day and age where a core idea as the foundational pillar on which brands are built seem irrelevant, our re-positioning for Baggit not only helped reinvigorate the brand but also paved the way for long term relevance. The #PlayTheLifeGame idea has not only helped trigger multiple hero campaigns but also became the basis for a larger creative and content effort for the brand. Most importantly, it helped meet the core objective of the exercise: helped re-launch Baggit as a fashion brand.

During our association with the brand, we supported the brand across multiple touch points including off-line, digital/e-commerce, social, retail/spatial, events, fashion weeks and promo led efforts. It was a full stack that took a holistic view to creative outcomes.

OVERALL BRAND IMPACT

Skechers crossed 1,000,000 pairs sold for the first time in the Indian market over the course of our partnership with the brand. Some of the properties we innovated continue to be marquee marketing events / IPs for the brand.
