Zoi offers curated global F&B experiences, blending unique gastronomy and mixology for the evolved Indian palette. Mixed into the experiences are immersive performances, live gigs, and more. Inspired by the Greek word for ‘Life’, we approached the engagement from the perspective of creating a brand that was visually delectable and contemporary.
