Essence

Luxury beach wear

Essence was a new-to-market, luxury beach wear brand that was launched for global markets. Designed with an international resort wear audience in mind the range included bikinis, sarongs, kaftans and fashion accessories. Our approach focused on bringing the message and medium to come together to tell a story. Inspired by the name Essence, we captured the ethos of the brand by putting it in its natural environment.
