Beautiful.

Kaveri’s design isn’t about ‘big bang’ or epiphany moments. Her design sensibilities lie in the nuances and subtleties of daily life. Built around the voice ‘Everyday Beautiful’, we gave the brand a unique identity & communication form that embraced a global idiom without losing its Indian roots.

Everyday Beautiful

We built the brand to be bigger than seasonal considerations. The presentation is all about pieces you want to touch and wear every day.

OVERALL BRAND IMPACT

Our work gave the brand a sophisticated and versatile program to create a national and global play. It continues to make strides in a sea of undifferentiated fashion labels.
